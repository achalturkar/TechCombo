Empowering Talent, Enabling Opportunities

Welcome to TechCombo, where we bridge the gap between skilled professionals and businesses in need of exceptional talent. With 5+ years of experience, we have become a trusted recruitment consultancy, known for connecting employers with top-notch candidates for freelance, contractual, and full-time roles across IT and non-IT sectors.

Our expertise lies in fostering meaningful connections between organizations and professionals, ensuring the right fit for every role. We offer a wide range of staffing solutions to meet the unique needs of start-ups, enterprises, and established companies. From project-based hiring to long-term placements, our mission is to create opportunities that unlock potential for both job seekers and businesses.

With every project, we aim to deliver value by connecting organizations with the right talent and providing job seekers with opportunities that align with their skills and aspirations.

Our Journey

2020

Founded in 2020, TechCombo began its journey with a focus on the IT sector, leveraging the growing demand for tech talent in a rapidly evolving digital landscape. Our commitment to connecting skilled professionals with innovative companies laid the foundation for our success, and we quickly established a reputation for delivering exceptional staffing solutions.

2022

In 2022, we took a bold step to diversify our offerings by expanding our services beyond the IT sector. Recognizing the growing demands and diverse needs of businesses across various industries, we successfully ventured into the non-IT sector. This strategic move allowed us to broaden our talent pool and offer comprehensive recruitment solutions to a wider range of clients.

2023

By 2023, we proudly celebrated the successful completion of projects for over 100 clients across multiple sectors. This milestone not only reflects our dedication to excellence but also underscores our ability to adapt to market demands and deliver results. Our journey has been marked by growth, innovation, and a relentless pursuit of connecting talent with opportunity.
